Optical simulator for magnetized plasma metallic photonic crystals

△

Photonic Crystals (PCs) are artificially made materials to harness the power of photons in applications such as cloaking, optical computers that could be a million times faster than electronics computers, optical fiber transmissions, and more. In this project, I developed algorithms and used them to simulate and study the optical properties of a subclass of PCs called 2D magnetized plasma metallic photonic crystals.
